Welcome to city of lake : Ranchi

Johar !!

Ranchi is a scenic town on the Chhota Nagpur Plateau, the capital of Jharkhand, and a gateway to explore the natural and cultural wonders of the state. Ranchi boasts of several waterfalls, such as the Dasham Falls, the Johna Falls, and the Hundru Falls, that offer refreshing views and picnic spots. The Birsa Zoological Park is a home for more than hundreds of species of animals and birds, while the Patratu Valley is a scenic drive with panoramic views of the hills and the dam. The Jagannath Temple, perched on a hilltop, is a historical landmark that resembles the famous Puri temple in Odisha. Ranchi has a pleasant climate throughout the year and offers a range of accommodation and dining options for tourists.
